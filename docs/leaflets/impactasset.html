The Macro Problem

The Intention–Action Gap

76% of people aspire to be fit.
48% actually exercise regularly.
11.5M record UK gym memberships, driven by Gen Z — yet nearly two-thirds of us still feel we don't look after our health enough.

The Hidden Barriers

Why aren't we going? For many, it's not a lack of desire. Rising rates of 'gymtimidation' — particularly among women and beginners — keep people away. The gym feels like a solo, intimidating chore rather than a welcoming space.

The Societal Cost

£7.4bn

Physical inactivity is associated with 1 in 6 deaths in the UK and costs the nation £7.4 billion every year — including £0.9 billion to the NHS alone.

Redefining Fitness as a Team Sport

GymJam doesn't just track workouts — it engineers accountability. By combining transparent social planning (the Squad Map), loss-aversion micro-stakes (the Pot), and tangible digital rewards (GymSpace), GymJam turns the isolated struggle of going to the gym into a collaborative, community-driven habit.
